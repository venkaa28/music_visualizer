The Music Visualizer

Introduction

Before we get started, here is some basic information about the Music Visualizer!

WARNING

This application is prone to causing seizures due to fast moving visuals and changing colors. If you are epileptic, please be mindful.

  • The on screen control menu will appear when your mouse is moved.
  • At the top of the screen, there is a selection of visualization scenes you can choose from by clicking on the desired chose.
  • Press the upload button with the cards icon on the right of the bar to select a source to visualize from, and press it again to turn this menu off.
  • Press the volume control button with the sliders icon on the right of the bar to manipulate the selected audio, and press it again to turn this menu off.
  • Press the profile icon button on the bottom right to go to the profile page, and click the music visualization button at the top of the screen to return to the visualization.
  • After you've linked your Spotify account, go into a device where Spotify is logged in and select 'MusicVisualizer' in Connect a Device
